The Mouse Series is a collection of pieces featuring my mouse characters. Several of them are from stories that have been on hold long enough that I wanted to release the images without the accompanying words – these are the circular paintings. Others are standalones or character studies, but every single one has a story.

The Garden Series began in the summer of 2013 and is an ongoing project. It began one afternoon at the kitchen sink as I rinsed a turnip from the garden and admired its nearly-perfect leaves – that turnip eventually made it into the soup pot, but not before it was drawn and painted. Each imaege is worked in watercolour and pencil from plants found growing in our yard, some wild, some cultivated.
