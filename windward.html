WindwardFlutes

For the last 10 years we have been supplying Windward Flutes with consulting, photography, and design services.

Windward has been a dream client, allowing us to explore avenues that were not always in the comfort realm of the customer, but we have grown side by side, helping develop each others skills and image. It is a true pleasure to work with a product that is so finely crafted and photogenic.

It should be disclosed that there is a family connection between our two businesses, Windward is owned by Sohier's parents. The business relationship has offered both of us an opportunity grow and explore together, and the work that we have produced for Windward stands on its own, from trade show displays, to business cards and web design.

You can view the website we built them by visiting windwardflutes.com.
