Weddings

You have spent months planning the perfect wedding, picking the perfect dress or suit and attending to every detail, now let us take care of beautifully documenting your special day. Every aspect of your big day is important, let us record them and hand them to you, full of wonderful memories, details and the friends and family that helped bring it together.

We will supply you with a private, online gallery of your wedding that you can share with friends and family, link images directly into Facebook or Google+ and order prints of any desired size or type easily and efficiently. We will also give you a flash drive with high res jpegs of all successful images, and post process a selection to perfection.
