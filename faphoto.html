fine art photography

Landscape photography was what got me into photography in the first place. When I moved to Nova Scotia I was lucky to be surrounded by so much beauty.

Black and White Photography

While attending NSCAD University I gained an appreciation for black and white photography and the patience it requires. Watching a print slowly appear had a profound impact on my art, and gave me an incredible appreciation for the flexibility and speed that modern technology has given us. I work almost exclusivly in the digital realm these days, but the way of seeing texture and light that is required when shooting film still guides my process to this day.

Sky and Sea

Living in Nova Scotia has given me access to an incredibly diverse and beautiful coastline, stunning skies and a desire to explore. I am drawn to sunrises, leaving the warmth of bed to venture onto our wild shores long before the sun starts to touch the sky. The moment when the suns rays first hit the clouds is one of the most peaceful and beautiful experiences I know of, and I hope that the final image carries a bit of that experience to the viewer.
