Weddings

You have spent months planning the perfect wedding, picking the perfect dress and attending to every detail, now let us take care of beautifully documenting your special day. Every aspect of your big day is important, let us record them and hand them to you in a beautiful leather bound picture book full of wonderful memories, details and the friends and family that helped bring it together.

We will supply you with a private, online gallery of your photographs, you can share this with your friends and family, link images directly into Facebook, or Google+ and order prints of any desired size or type easily and efficiently.

Wedding Schedule

Initial Consultation
We will come to you to discuss your requirements, pricing, and to go through some examples of the images you want. Please feel free to have a collection of image ideas that really stand out to you, we can use these as inspiration for the image style. We will discuss what extent of coverage you want, your budget, and what
Rehearsal
We will meet you at the venue a few days beforehand, scout out places to take group photos, and select the important areas you want to capture most. This walkthrough will help us determine what equipment we will need, and allow us to prepare

A few days before the wedding
Day of the Wedding
Depending on how much of the preparation you want photographed we will arrive as early as you need. Depending on our earlier meetings we can stay for the entire duration of the reception and dance, or just for the ceremony, its up to you and your budget.

Proofing and Recieving
Within a day or two of your wedding we will send you a few of our favorite images that we captured, the important ones. We will require at least 5 business days to sort the images and prepare to have a consultation and proofing session. This will usually take about two hours to select your favorite images (depending on the package you buy this could be anywhere from 30 images to 150).

4-6 Days after the wedding

Wedding FAQs

Where are you located?

We live just outside of Halifax, Nova Scotia, in the lovely community of West Chezzetcook. We are willing to travel, be it to the city, or out of province.

Do you charge a travel fee?

If we have to drive outside of HRM, we charge 0.50/km. We are more than willing to work destination weddings with our travel/accommodation covered. If your out-of-town wedding would require us to stay a night in a hotel, we would include that in our fee as well. However, if we have friends in your area or feel that we can make the drive home safely, we’ll do so to avoid the charge.

How many hours of shooting does our fee cover?

Generally, we assume 8 hours. Your official quote will reflect the length of time you would like, which can be as short as simply covering the wedding ceremony and photos with family (4 hours), to recording the events of your entire day. We like to start with the pre-wedding prep, and leave after the dance has started.

Can we book you for our engagement photos as well?

Please do! It provides both of us a great opportunity to get to know one another better. We can more easily read how comfortable you are in front of the camera and if you’re the type who is camera shy, it’s a great chance to relax as you see what we do without the pressure of all your friends and family being present. We find it so useful, that if you book us as your wedding photographers, the engagement session becomes free.

Do we need to sign a contract to book you?

Absolutely, no exceptions. A contract is in both of our best interests.

When can we expect our photos?

We like to send out a couple of our favourite shots to you the day after your ceremony. They will be uploaded to a password-protected site which you are free to share with friends. The rest of your photos will be made available online as soon as possible after your wedding - this varies according to the season as summer is a busy time! Officially, you can expect your online gallery to be fully ready in 6 to 8 weeks, but it’s usually quicker than that. We will consult after you’ve had the chance to go over the gallery to ensure that the images we send to you on the flashdrive are final edits. Obviously, if we need to mail your photos to you, that adds to the delivery time.

What ownership do you retain of our images?

As per Federal law, we retain the copyright to all photos we take in the same way a painter retains the rights to an image she’s painted. In a nutshell, what this means is that we can use images of your wedding for promotional purposes, but we choose to always seek permission first out of respect for your privacy. This includes hosting pictures as an example of past jobs on our website for future clients to see and submitting shots to photography contests/wedding magazines/blogs and the like. We are also happy to sell prints to you and your guests. Of course, we can always discuss and come to a solution that leaves everyone happy if you are uncomfortable with us using images of your day for promotional purposes.

So… does that mean there are things I can’t do with my photos?

Yes, but they’re all pretty common-sense things. You can certainly share images of your wedding with friends and family as you’d expect, you can print off photos for personal use, and build your own wedding album as a few examples - you simply can’t profit from them. It also means that you cannot submit those photos to contests/wedding magazines/blogs and so on (if you’d like to do those things, just let us know and we’d be more than happy to submit them for you). It also means that since we hold copyright, we ask that you please not edit any images - that’s what we’re here for and that service is included in the fee you pay. Just let us know if you’d like a particular treatment - it’s our job, after all!

What kind of photo editing do you typically do?

As little as possible. The shots are already well-composed, so our main task in editing is colour-balancing and reducing the high number of photos to a manageable gallery for you by removing doubles. We will occasionally fine-tune a photo that say, had a stranger rambling through the background, but we do not feel that extensive editing is necessary, or desired, in most cases. We will never assume and photoshop someone’s appearance, but if you’ve been bitten by a monster mosquito and would like that bug bite on your forehead edited out please let us know - we will not do this without your request.

Can you print our pictures for us or design a wedding album?

You bet! You’ll be able to purchase prints in many different sizes, formats, and on different media right from the online gallery where they’ll be hosted. As for the album, we definitely can produce an album for you.

Would you like a shot list?

We already have a pretty solid basic shot list which we will go over with you at our meeting. What we need from you are details if the family shots will deviate from the standard - for example, if the groom’s parents are divorced and would rather take separate shots with their new partners, if someone’s grandmother has a condition which means she should be in the first round of pictures so that she can leave to rest, etc… Just what, exactly, “the standard” is is something we will be certain to cover with you.

I’ve seen some really neat pictures out there and I want to recreate a few. Can we do that?

Suggestions are always welcome - what we want is to make you happy! Bearing that in mind, it always helps to really take a good look at the picture and imagine it in your chosen venue - is it possible? We can always recreate the actions, but sometimes certain shots just don’t have the same appeal if the setting is drastically different. That being said, once all the official photos are done (and they are important - when was the last time your entire family was in one spot at the same time?) we encourage you to have fun.

When should we book?

As soon as you feel ready - we recommend at least 12 months in advance for weddings as summer dates can fill up especially quickly! We’d also love the time to be able to fit in an engagement session with you. We would prefer at least a month in advance for other shoots. If you’re in a bind it doesn’t hurt to ask! We may have an opening as we don’t publicise our schedule.

What is the deposit and when is it due?

Your deposit varies depending on the service (it will be listed under the service’s details), and is due when you book. The deposit is non-refundable and guarantees that we hold that date for you as once you book, we will turn down all other jobs for that particular day. The rest can be paid at your convenience, though no later than a week before for weddings and the day of the shoot for other services, please.

How can we pay?

We accept cash, cheques or e-transfers.
