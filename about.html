About us

We are Sohier and Sarah Christie, a husband and wife creative team. We met while attending NSCAD University in Halifax, Nova Scotia and have developed a passion for embarking on challenges and turning our visions into reality. We have both worked in the creative and print industry and have learned many skills that have helped us grow to cover a broad spectrum of diciplines. Our free time is spent tending our gardens, and creating art in many different mediums.
